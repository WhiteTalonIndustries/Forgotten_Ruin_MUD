# Help

Welcome to Forgotten Ruin MUD!

This is a MUD based on the "Forgotten Ruin" book series. You are a US Army Ranger, transported 10,000 years into the future to a world where fantasy is real. You are not alone, you control a full squad of Rangers.

Here are some help topics to get you started:

*   **squad:** Learn about how to command your squad.
*   **combat:** Understand the combat system.
*   **skills:** Learn about the skill-based progression system.
*   **world:** Get a brief overview of the world of Ruin.
